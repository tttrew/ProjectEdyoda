The Great Wall of China

The only structure that can be spotted from space, the longest structure
to be built by man. Yes! I am talking about an absolutely outstanding piece of
architecture, I am talking about the Great Wall of China. A feat of building only rivalled by the Great
Pyramids this mega structure goes on for lengthof 29,196 kilometres accross the hills of China. The sheer
length and volume of this structure is so great that there is no word invented that could describe it perfectly.

If you are not into architecture, you might think that this absolute beauty
is none of your business, but sorry you are wrong probably beacause you have not seen the exceptional view of
the Chines hillside fromthe surface of the wall, yes architecture is not all that it offers!

This is a structure of absolutely no match, it's a must visit!
