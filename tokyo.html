Tokyo,Japan

Tokyo, the capital city of Japan gives us theglimpse of what all cities would look like in the future. It is
deffinitely a role model for any city, it has perfect and picturesque urban landscape with notable
inclusions of the Giant Tokyo Tower and the intensely futuristic Rainbow Bridge, it is packed with buildings
yet is the most beautiful city you will see.

Do not let it's Urban landscape fool you though, Tokyo is stiil the perfect combination of natural and
artificial. Its beautiful green trees, spread out accross its face just contribute to its natural beauty, but
that is not all, you have to visit this city in the Spring, the season of he magnificent'Cherry Blossom'
when the trees turn to a brilliant shade of pink contributing to its stunning look.

And I haven't yet talked about the food, the Japanese cuisine is one that you must try out, with the normal
noddles and Sushi, tou must also try out the less known dishes of the cuisine for example the magnificent
Teppanyaki, the brilliant Yakiniku and the tasty Katsudon, and you will just forget about any other cuisine!

It's a place that you must visit! So pack your bags its time for a great outing in Japan!
