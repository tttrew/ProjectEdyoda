Maldives

What would you expect from an island country? Beaches? Well that is exactly what maldives gives us with its
stunning, eye-catching and clean beaches. Touted as the cleanest beaches in the world, the beaches of Maldives
are picture perfect with their soft-brown sand and magnificent blue seas surrounding the whole Island Nation.

But that is not all, the coral reefs underwater, and the cleanliness of seas contribute to a diversity of
marine organisms living under the surface of the water. Sea-turtles? Goldfish? Dolphins? Name what you want to
see and I assure you that it will most definitely be there in the seas of the Maldives.

The island country of Maldives is one that you must visit, so what are you waiting for? Book your tickets.
