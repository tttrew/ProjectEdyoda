London, England

London is the city that might be the oldest in the whole world but perfectly incorporates the element of old
and new, a perfect comination of time a collection of fragments from different centuries put together into
one city and that makes it look absolutely beautiful, gorgeous and most of all, stunning and fabulous.

It has buildings like the Buckingham Palace, the Big Ben and the London Bridge all which have seen and
endured centuries, but don't let that make you think that London is a city from a black and white film, it also
contains architectural modern marvels like the London Eye, and the beautiful and stunning stadiums at
Wembley & Tottenham.

London is a city of class, the city of Isaac Newton has much to offer for travellers like us, it's a city
that you just cannot help but visit, from the stadium of Lord's to Westminister Abbey London is a city of
architecture and buildings, but a city marvellous and fantastic built over 700 years!
