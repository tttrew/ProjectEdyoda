The Grand Canyon, Arizona, U.S.A

Of all the gradual events which have modified the Earth's surface over millions and billions of years none
has had and effect or has produced results as beautiful and breathtaking likethe Grand Canyon, if it was done
by man, it would've been nothing short of genius, but the work of nature, or the colorado river more
specifically, to cut open the rocks of northern Arizona is simply magnificent.

Ranging from 655 feet to 18 miles in width and about 5135 feet at its deepest, the rock formations are
absolutely beautiful, filled with rocks of all kinds with different colors these exposed layers of rock look stunning
looks at both sunrise and sunset it is simply a masterpiece!

The rewards of hard work must be given, and nature has definitely worked really hard to present us with this
beautiful sight, so visit and gulp down the pleasure it gives you
