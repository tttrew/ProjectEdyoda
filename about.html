About Me!

Travel is an inseparable part of our lives, we have to move, experience new things, meet new people and most
importantly to see new places. Travel has been simply hardwired into brains by nature, since the days of
hunting and gathering in East Africa. Man just can't help but move!

I'm a person, who just accepts this part of travel, I love experiencing new things, I just love moving
around from place to place, country to country. To be always in the hunt for new places and new experiences it
is an inseparable part of who I am. Travel is an important part of my life. A part that allows me to switch
off and simply get a break.

But it is not all about me I plan to get everyone to discover their own love for travel, that's why I'm
suggesting these places so that every single person can find their love! That is my aim, that is who I am.
