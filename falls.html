Niagara Falls, Canada

'A sight to behold', 'A sight like no other','Unbelievable' are words that will come to your mouth when you
land your eyes on this marvel of nature, the ultimate sight, it is just outstanding. Crafted by nature over
hundreds of years, the Niagara falls on the borders of Canada and the United States looks like something from
heaven.

It mightnot be the biggest Waterfall or even the highest, lower in height to many water falls accross the
globe, but I assure you it is the most magnificent of them all at a height of 157 feet the sheer volume of water
coming down from it is enough to send chills down your spine. That's not it though the large, loud yet sweet
noise coming from the bellows of these great waterfall is enough to make you want to visit it again and
again.

It is a sight to behold and a sight that you should definitely not miss out on, a place you have to visit,
it's a place of beauty, it is the Niagara falls.
